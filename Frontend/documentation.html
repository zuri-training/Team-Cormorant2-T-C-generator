Problem Statement

For many businesses and start-ups, integrating conditional
agreements on their websites can be difficult due to the
time-consuming process of obtaining agreements and
the cost associated with doing so.

Solution

Terms and Conditions can only serve as a safeguard for the
owner if they are flawless. We are aware that the traditional
method of drafting your own terms and conditions can be
tedious and time-consuming.As a result, we have made an
effort to provide potential users with a variety of options
from which they can automatically generate an agreement
that will meet that intended goal in the fastest time possible,
by inputting the required information about their businesses.
